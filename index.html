About

Just a short story about us

  • Mar 2021

    LetsWork is Born

    We are here to help those of you who don't want to go to the office but can still work comfortably. We have many branches, and spread in every city. So you don't have to worry about distance issues. We also offer the same facilities as what your office has.

  • Jan 2021

    But, There Is a Problem

    When working from home, we tend to get distracted. The disturbance can come from anywhere, such as house renovation, a crying baby, suddenly slow internet, and so on.

  • Jan 2021

    Transition to New Normal

    By this time, we are all used to working from home. We only need a computer and fast internet. So, many employees do not feel the need to go to the office. Moreover, the distance from home to the office makes employees reluctant to go to the office.

  • Mar 2020

    Covid-19 Outbreak

    This is a troubled time. We all have to go into quarantine. Every aspect of life changes drastically. Many workers have to adapt to be able to work from home.
